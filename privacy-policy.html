This Privacy Policy explains how information may be collected, used, shared and protected when you use Kurnex. This application is developed and published by Kurnex Tech.

1. Information We Collect

We collect limited information only when it is necessary to provide, maintain and improve the application. The data may include:

Advertising partners may collect or process information such as device identifiers, usage information, ad impressions, clicks and engagement data. These services may use cookies or similar technologies where applicable to provide personalized or non-personalized advertisements and measure ad performance.

Users can manage advertising preferences from their device settings, including resetting or limiting the advertising identifier where supported.

The application may offer premium features through in-app purchases. Purchases are securely processed by platform providers such as Google Play and Apple App Store.

We do not collect or store payment card numbers, bank details or billing credentials. Refunds, cancellations and subscription management are handled according to the policies of the relevant platform provider.

Collected information may be used to:

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent user data. Limited data may be shared only with service providers required for app functionality, advertising, analytics, payment processing, security or legal compliance.
